The Challenge

Six problems one platform can't solve alone

These aren't Microsoft failures — they're architectural constraints of building AI on top of siloed business applications.

🧱

The Brick Wall

Sales Hub and Customer Service Hub access the same Dataverse but don't share context. Field reps can't see what inside sales is processing. Orders ship without pipeline visibility.

Revenue Risk
📱

Mobile Is an Afterthought

Field reps live on phones, not desktops. The D365 mobile app is a mismatch of legacy and modern tech. If reps can't use it from the car, they won't use it at all.

Adoption Blocker
🚫

No Data, No AI

Copilot features exist but can't function without structured data in the right fields. When reps resist data entry, the entire AI investment goes dark.

Critical Dependency
🔀

Multi-Reseller Blind Spots

Three resellers quoting for one end customer. Dynamics ties opportunities to pricing and trade agreements, making parallel reseller tracking problematic.

Complexity Gap
🏛️

IT Governance Bottleneck

CRM changes go through ERP-grade approval processes. Marketing can't tweak an email flow without navigating mission-critical governance designed for financial systems.

Speed Constraint
📚

Knowledge Base Friction

KB articles should auto-generate from closed cases. In practice, it requires manual creation, review, and approval — a pipeline that compounds delay.

In Practice

What this looks like day-to-day

Real scenarios based on how your teams actually work — not theoretical features.

🚗

Post-Visit Capture

A field sales rep leaves a customer meeting. Instead of opening an app and filling forms, they speak naturally about what happened. OSCortex captures the notes, updates the opportunity, adjusts the close date, and logs the activity — all from a voice interaction in the car.

"Just left Midwich. Good meeting with Sarah — they're moving forward on the mic package. Bump the close date to end of month and mark it at 75% confidence."
🔗

Order-Pipeline Sync

An inside sales agent processes an order. OSCortex recognises it matches an open opportunity being worked by a field rep and automatically notifies them. No more discovering a deal closed without your knowledge.

"Heads up — Canford Audio just placed the Manchester University order you've been working. 22K confirmed. Your opportunity has been updated."
📊

BDM Daily Dashboard

The unified view Gary described: every customer interaction across sales, support, quotes, and orders — visible in one place. No Power BI build required. Just ask.

"Show me everything that happened with my top 10 accounts this week — quotes, orders, support tickets, and any field activity."
🎪

Trade Show Follow-Up

Upload leads from ISE or Plasa. OSCortex runs the entire sequence — thank-you email within 24 hours, personal follow-up nudge at day 3, phone call reminder at day 7 — without building sequences in Sales Accelerator.

"We picked up 45 leads at Plasa. Run the standard trade show follow-up. Assign UK leads to the regional reps, Benelux leads to the Benelux team."
🧠

Continuous Knowledge Base

Every resolved support case automatically becomes institutional knowledge. No manual article creation, no approval queue. When a similar issue comes in, the system already knows the answer and drafts the response.

"We resolved a camera troubleshooting case last month. A similar query just came in — here's the suggested response based on what worked before."
Beyond CRM

What Microsoft will never do

CRM is plumbing. The real competitive edge is what happens on top of it — creative, personalised, impossible-to-ignore interactions that make your sales team unforgettable.

🎵

Personalised Client Songs

Your clients are in music. Imagine a follow-up that arrives not as another templated email, but as a custom-written song — lyrics built from the client's history, their venue, their upcoming events, performed in a style that matches their world. Generated automatically from CRM data. No human copywriter needed.

"Hey Gary, thanks for the Plasa meeting — here's a little something we made for the Arches Social." Attached: a 60-second track with lyrics referencing their mic package request and venue name. Try ignoring that in your inbox.
🎬

AI Video Proposals

Instead of a PDF quote, send a 90-second personalised video walkthrough — the product in context, specs narrated for their specific use case, pricing overlaid. Generated from the opportunity data your reps already entered. Every prospect gets a bespoke pitch without the production cost.

A venue asks about installed sound. Instead of a spec sheet, they get a narrated video showing their exact room type with recommended mic placement, speaker positioning, and a tailored quote — generated in minutes.
🎨

Dynamic Event Collateral

Trade show coming up? OSCortex generates personalised invitations for every contact in the pipeline — custom messaging based on their product interests, purchase history, and relationship stage. Not mail-merge. Genuinely different content for each recipient.

ISE is in two weeks. 200 leads get 200 different invitations — each referencing their specific interest area, last interaction with Audio Technica, and a reason to visit the stand that's actually relevant to them.
📊

Competitive Intelligence Briefs

Before every client meeting, the system generates a live competitive brief — what rivals are pricing, what's being spec'd on recent tenders, social sentiment around competing products. Your rep walks in with market intelligence that took zero research time.

"Morning briefing: Midwich meeting at 2pm. Yamaha dropped pricing on the DZR series last week. Three recent installs in Manchester used Shure over AT. Here's the spec comparison and your pricing advantage on the 40 Series."
🎤

Voice-Powered Demo Follow-Ups

A rep demos a product at a client site. On the drive home, they tell OSCortex what happened. The system automatically generates a follow-up package — a thank-you email with product links, a summary of what was discussed, a quote draft, and calendar hold for the next touchpoint. All from a 2-minute voice note.

"Just demoed the AT5045 at Royal Northern College of Music. They loved it. They want to compare against the 4050. Send them both spec sheets, a comparison summary, and a quote for six units. Follow up Thursday."
🧲

Win-Back Campaigns That Actually Work

A client goes quiet. Instead of a generic "we miss you" email, OSCortex analyses their full history — what they bought, when they last engaged, what products have launched since — and crafts a genuinely relevant re-engagement. Could be a personalised product recommendation, a curated playlist of new releases relevant to their setup, or an invitation that references something specific.

"Haven't heard from The Warehouse Project in 6 months. Last order was DT 700 Pro X headphones for their DJ booth. Allen & Heath just released the Xone:PX5 — that's a direct fit. Draft a personal note from their rep with the launch info and an offer to demo on-site."

Microsoft builds tools for managing data. OSCortex builds tools for winning business. Every interaction above is generated automatically from the data your team already has — no creative department, no agency, no manual effort.
